# Corona Renderer Suite — Portable Photorealistic Rendering Environment

## About Corona Renderer Suite

Corona Renderer Suite provides a complete physically‑based rendering engine for architectural visualization, product design, and VFX. Corona Renderer Suite combines unbiased Monte Carlo ray tracing, adaptive sampling (noise threshold 0.5‑5%), and AI denoising (Intel Open Image Denoise, NVIDIA OptiX, CPU/Fast Preview). Corona Renderer Suite supports LightMix interactive post‑processing (adjust color, intensity, temperature of lights and environment during or after rendering, save lightmix setup to .conf), volumetric scattering (fog, volume material, absorption, scattering anisotropy), and caustics (photons, fast caustics solver, from sun/sky and artificial lights). Corona Renderer Suite integrates material layering system (blend layers with masks, coat layer (clearcoat, roughness, IOR), displacement (2D/3D, screen size 1‑10px, max subdiv 256), decal maps (sticker, logo, label with falloff), procedural edge wear (round corners, chamfer, dirt in crevices, dust on horizontal surfaces)), PBR workflow (albedo, roughness, metalness, normal, opacity, translucency, SSS, emission, clearcoat), and integrated plugins for 3ds Max, Cinema 4D (S24, 2024, 2025, 2026), and other DCCs. Corona Renderer Suite enables users to create photorealistic stills, animations, interactive render (IR), render elements (beauty, reflection, refraction, diffuse, direct, indirect, caustics, volume, opacity, normal, depth, mask, material ID, object ID, wire color, texture map, light select), and export to EXR (OpenEXR 16/32‑bit), PNG, TIFF, JPEG, and HDR formats. Corona Renderer Suite runs on standard business workstations. Corona Renderer Suite is trusted by architectural visualizers, interior designers, product renderers, VFX artists, automotive designers, and marketing studios worldwide.

## Deep Dive — Corona Renderer Suite Notes
Adaptive sampling (noise level limit (0.5‑5%), pass limit (5‑2000), time limit 30‑3600 sec, denoise after completion, denoise blend 0‑100%)
LightMix (layers (sun, sky, environment, sphere, rectangle, disc, line, mesh, IES), enable color, intensity multiplier 0‑10, temperature 1000‑20000K, shadow color)
Material layering (base layer, coat (clearcoat, thickness 0‑100%, roughness 0‑100%, IOR 1.0‑2.5), blend by mask (bitmap, procedural, edge detection, curvature, AO, normal map), decal (project from screen or gizmo)
Displacement (screen size 2‑10px, max subdiv 32‑256, round geometry, minimal edge length 1‑5mm, displacement map intensity 0‑500mm, vector displacement support)
